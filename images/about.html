About the Series

Time Riders is a Series of teen science fiction novels written by Alex Scarrow. The series has nine books and is currently published by Puffin Books.

The series follows three teenagers who are recruited by an agency that prevents people from traveling back in time and changing history. The agency is based in New York City in the year 2001 and is equipped with a time machine that allows them to travel back in time to stop time travelers from changing history.
